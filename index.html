About

This site was created as a tribute to sir Terry Pratchett. Its goal is incredibly simple: to display a different quote from his books or statements every day.

If you like the idea of reminding yourself about the work of Sir Terry, consider subscribing to the mailing list in the form below.

The quotes from sir Terry come from "The Pratchett Quote File", which is a collection of quotes created and maintained by users.
